Misconception

Mental health issues can't affect me.

Everyone can be affected by mental health issues. In 2020, 52.9 millions U.S. adults experienced a mental health condition.

People with mental health issues are violent.

People with mental health issues are not likely to be violent as only 3-5% violent act are associated with mental health. Rather of them being suspect of violent, they are more likely to be victim of violent crime.

"I can't do anything for a person with a mental health issue."

Yes, you can! You can influence someone with mental health problem to get treatment and support them emotional along the way.
