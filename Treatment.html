What can you/someone do if you/someone experiencing mental health conditions?

  • Ask for help from someone you trusted and know and keep in touch with that person when things get rough.
  • Getting enough sleep allow your mind, mood, and wellbeing to improve.
  • Text ‘988’ or chat online on 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline’s website for free and immediate help. In addition, you can call 1-800-662-HELP (4357) to access the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration 24/7 helpline.
