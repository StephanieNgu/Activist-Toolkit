Your Mental Health Matters

Taking care of your mental health is as important as taking care of your health's physical and emotional wellbeing. The way you feel, think, and act will be affected by your mental health.

Who is affected by it?

Everyone is affected by it. It could start from childhood to adulthood. In the US, the highest age group rate experiencing mental health conditions are young adult from 18-25. While individuals who are multiracial are the highest group experiencing mental health conditions.
